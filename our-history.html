OUR JOURNEY

From a small startup in Kerala, we have grown steadily through dedication, trust, and a strong commitment to quality. Our focus on customer satisfaction and reliable service helped us expand and build a solid presence in the automotive spare parts industry. Today, we proudly serve as an official Shell Lubricants distributor, continuing our journey with the same passion for excellence and innovation.

The Beginning

2017

Incorporated in a remote area of Kerala with the vision of expanding its network throughout the entire state in automotive spare parts industry.

First Operations

2018

With just three employees, the company started operations focusing on the two nearest districts and achieved a sustainable turnover.

Expansion Phase

2020

The company's employee base grew to 15 as it expanded its network to five districts and saw a significant rise in sales.

Digital Transformation

2022

To support growing sales and logistics operations across five districts, the company implemented SAP Business One and also added an additional warehouse.

Continued Success

2023

Celebrating continued success by expanding the network to 10 districts, adding another warehouse, and growing the team to 36 employees.

Market Diversification

2024

To meet growing customer demand, the company expanded its warehouse capacity to 5,000 sq. ft., strengthened its management team, and entered the lubricants and oils market.

Major Milestone

2025

As a milestone in our operational growth, we are proud to become an official distributor of Shell, a global leader in oils and lubricants.
